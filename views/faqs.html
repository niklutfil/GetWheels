    Frequently Ask Questions (FAQs)

  1. How can I make a booking?
    Booking can made by website. For booking, you can go to booking forms and fill up all the information needed.
  2. What are the steps and documents I need to rent with GetWheels Transportation?
    • A valid license driver
    • Original IC
    • Student visa or pass (id needed)
  3. What are the age requirement to rent a car
    All hires must be the age of 20 and above with a valid CDL (Competent Driving License). No probationary license accepted.
  4. Do GetWheels Transportation Cover Fuel Expenses?
    No, we do not cover fuel expenses through out your journey. We are practising fuelto-policy in which renter are always liable to return the car with the same level of fuel given to renter during pickup.
  5. Does the car comes with full fuel?
    No. Fuel level may vary depending on availability during pickup.
  6. I have caught speeding. How much do I need to pay?
    You are always liable to pay for the compasation of company losses, including traffic fine and summons imposed by PDRM or JPJ not limited to any offences.
  7. Can I bring my own car seat?
    Yes, you may bring your own child seat with a condition while installing it to GetWheels 's car transportation.
  8. How many days in advance at minimum do I need to book my car?
    The minimum you need to book is 24 hours subject to availability. However, we recommend booking earlier to avoid disappointment. You can also secure a better rate when you book early with us.
