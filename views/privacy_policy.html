Privacy Policy

At GetWheels Transportation Service, we priotize the protection of your privacy and are dedicated to maintaining the confidentiality of the personal information you entrust to us. Our collection of your personal data is intended for the purpose of delivering our transportation services, future marketing endeavors, and overall service enhancement.

Rest assured that access to your personal information is restricted to authorized personnel within our organization only. We uphold a strict policy of not disclosing customer information to third parties, except when it is imperative for the provision of a service to you. This may include activities such as coordinating product delivery, conducting credit and security checks, or engaging in customer research and profiling initiatives.

In specific instances, we may be obligated to share this information with regulatory bodies, legal authorities, auditors, affiliated companies, third-party service providers, and designated marketing agencies. Be assured that such disclosures are made in accordance with relevent laws and regulations, ensuring the utmost protection of your personal data.

Your Consent: We affirm that we will never trade, sell or disclose your name, address, email address, credit card information, or any personal details to any external party, except in cases where you have explicitly granted permission or have established a connection with partners linked to our website.

Communication & Marketing: Upon renting a car with us, we may periodcally update you on our latest services, news and special offers via email, postal mail, or telephone. As a member of GetWheels Transportations Service, you have the option to opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us and selected third parties.

To unsubscribe, visit 'Your Details' in 'Your Account' on our website after registration, or click the 'unsubscribe' link in any email we send you.

Contact Us: We value your feedback and any inquiries. If you have any questions about this policy, want to stop information processing, or need assistance, contact the WAGON CAR RENTAL SERVICES team. We appreciate your time and any refferals to friends and family.
